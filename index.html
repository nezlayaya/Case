Features

Offset (iQx™)

The well plan is broken down into a series of operational phases and sub-divided into events.

Experience (iQx™)

All the costs that relate to the well being planned are captured. The costs are assigned to individual or a range of phases.

Geology (iQx™)

P1™ allows contingency events to be entered. Contingencies can be applied to individual or a group of phases.

TIME (iQx™)

The Process Flow Map provides a visual representation of the sequence of events that make up the P1™ model

Probabilistic Modelling (P1™)

P1™ supports multiple currencies which allows costs to be entered in a native currency before converting and reporting on a project currency

Risk Management (P1™)

Using the information entered in the Cost Model, P1™ can generate a probabilistic AFE.

3D Visualising (P1™)

Сurve for time/cost – Using the results of Monte Carlo simulation, P1™ generates cumulative probability charts for both time and cost.

Cost Control (CT™)

Phase duration charts allow the team to indentify the phase(s) that have high inherent risk. By focusing attention and effort to these areas risk can be mitigated, reduced or even eliminated

Managing Supplies (M2™)

By recording the actual duration of an event, analysis can be performed on the model to compare Planned versus Actual performance. Such analysis can aid the team by identifying opportunities for training, lessons learned or even good practice.

Data Analytics

Data analytics will only be successful if companies have complete understanding and plan of how they will gain value from their raw data. iQx can import data from multiple sources including automatic recognition of industry standard data formats. Old reports that have been scanned can be imported into iQx data base by passing through Optical Character Recognition (OCR) functionality and analysed with specific algorithms that extract the required data into a digital format.

As a result, combining historic and real time data by applying iQx for analytics can benefit engineering teams in finding trends, making predictions, calculating time and cost.
